About the game

"Weeezaa Board game" is a modified version of real
Ouija board. Ouija boards can be fun, but they’re not
for everyone. Many find a thrill in spookiness and the
supernatural. People who enjoy scary movies appreciate
the feeling of excitement horror creates. So, if there’s
someone in the group losing their sh*t during a seance, be considerate and play something else.
